Cloud Computing - 
Cloud Computing is an advance way of accessing the services like CPU, websites, servers and many more virtually.
There are mainly three services 
Iaas - Infrastructure as a service
Pass - Platform as a Service
Saas - Software as a service
cloud computing involves services like -
-storage
-compute Servers
-web Hosting
-security to data 

PAAS - stands for platform as a service 
this service provides platform for various reasons like development of web applications.
EC2 is one of the best example of Paas
